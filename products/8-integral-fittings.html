Integral Fittings

Product Descriptions

SLETPETRO integral fittings are forged from high-strength alloy steel and serve as key flow-distributing components in manifold connections. Multiple crack detection processes reduce manufacturing defects.

To minimize size and weight, engineers performed finite element stress analysis on each fitting body. Inner bores are chamfered to reduce stress in tubular intersecting joints.

SLETPETRO provides a wide range of high-pressure integral fittings, including tees, laterals, wyes, crosses, elbows, long-radius elbows, adapters, and more. Customized designs are also available.

Product Specifications

  • Nominal Size: 2–4
  • Temperature Rating: −29°C to 121°C (PU)
  • Service: Standard
  • Customization: Available
