Hose Loop Overview

Hose Loops are mainly used for transporting fluid in cementing, well testing and high pressure discharge line. They are composed of swivel joints and straight pipes, which are used to optimize the installation and distribution of pipeline, and can be easily and conveniently folded up during storage and transportation. SLETPETRO Hose loops can come in sizes and configurations to meet any need.
