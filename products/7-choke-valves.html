Choke Valve Overview

Choke valves are used to control flow rate and pressure for processing of produced fluids in cementing and well testing. The critical components of SLETPETRO choke valves are forged from high strength alloy steel with superior abrasive resistance. The spring energized seal structure of stem provides unsurpassed reliability.
