Hydraulic Actuator

Product Introduction

Hydraulic double-acting balanced rotary actuator

This is a 90° quarter-turn hydraulic double-acting balanced rotary actuator, designed for precise control and reliable performance in demanding environments.

Product Features

Technical Parameters

  • Working pressure: 30–135 bar
  • Burst test pressure: 675 bar
  • Total weight: 20 kg
  • Oil displacement: 0.21 L
  • Rotation angle: 90° ± 1°

Operating Temperature Range

  • Normal temperature: -25°C to +80°C (nitrile rubber)
  • High temperature: -20°C to +120°C (fluororubber)
  • Low temperature: -45°C to +40°C

Oil Requirements

We recommend using acid-free hydraulic oil with a viscosity range of 15–200 cSt. For recommended brands and for media other than oil, please refer to a separate data sheet.
