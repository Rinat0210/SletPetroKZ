Pup Joint Overview

SLETPETRO pup joints are forged from superior alloy steel with partial heat treating process, which are widely used for transporting high pressure flow in fracturing line, cementing line and NACE Service. Pup joints have been optimized for minimum weight and size to extend service life, easy to transport and install on site.

Product Specifications

  • Nominal Size: 2~4
  • Temperature Rating: -29℃~121℃ (PU)
  • Service: Standard
  • Connections: Unions and Flange End Connections
