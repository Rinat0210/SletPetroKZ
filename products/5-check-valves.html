Check Valves

Product Descriptions

Check valves are used in high-pressure lines to control one-way flow and prevent backflow in pipelines, protecting both the pipeline and equipment.

The core component is forged from stainless steel with high resistance to erosion and abrasion. Seals use secondary vulcanization for enhanced sealing performance.

SLETPETRO provides top-entry check valves, in-line flapper check valves, and dart check valves. Flapper valves are used for fluid or fluid-solid mixtures, while dart valves are suitable for gas or low-viscosity fluids.

Product Specifications

  • Nominal Size: 2–3
  • Temperature Rating: −29°C to 121°C (PU)
  • Service: Standard, Sour Gas
  • Manufacturing Standard: API 6A & NACE MR0175
