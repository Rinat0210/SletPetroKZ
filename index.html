SletPetro KZ LLP is a Kazakhstan-based supplier of high-pressure oilfield equipment, supporting drilling, well servicing, and hydraulic fracturing operations across the region.

The company delivers a focused range of products engineered for demanding environments, including high-pressure manifold systems, valves, swivel joints, and related components. All equipment is manufactured to meet industry requirements for strength, reliability, and safe operation under extreme pressure conditions.

The company works in close partnership with the manufacturer Sletpetro, ensuring direct supply from the production source and providing clients with consistent quality and dependable delivery timelines.

SletPetro KZ LLP is committed to:

  • reliable product supply for critical oilfield operations
  • clear and responsive communication with clients
  • practical solutions tailored to field requirements

With a growing presence in Kazakhstan, the company focuses on building long-term partnerships with oil and gas operators, service companies, and contractors.

Our Products

1. Hydraulic Actuator

A 90° double-acting rotary actuator for precise control in demanding environments.

2. Suction Buffer

Absorbs fracturing fluid impact using gas to stabilize suction flow and protect pump components.

3. Plug Valves

Compact, low-torque valves essential for well fracturing and cementing operations.

4. Swivel Joints

Forged alloy steel joints with triple ball races for durable pipeline protection.

5. Check Valves

High-pressure valves designed to control one-way flow and prevent backflow damage.

6. Pressure Relief Valves

Spring-reset valves that automatically open to protect against line overpressure.

7. Choke Valves

High-strength alloy valves used to control flow rates and pressure during testing.

8. Integral Fittings

Forged flow-distributing components like tees and crosses for manifold connections.

9. Pup Joints

Heat-treated alloy steel pipes optimized for transporting high-pressure flow.

10. Hose Loops

Flexible configurations of swivels and pipes for easy installation and transport.

11. Reel Swivels

Connects coiled tubing to surface lines, enabling rotation under pressure at job sites.

12. Manifold Skids

Combines high and low-pressure components to gather and pump fluids to the wellhead.

13. Valve Lubricant Gun

High-pressure tools for quick, easy grease injection during valve maintenance.
